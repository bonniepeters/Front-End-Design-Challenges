Lodging

Tacoma + Pierce County Mountain - City - Sea

With your feet in the salt water of Tacoma's Commencement Bay, the 14,411-foot peak of Mount Rainier is just 42 miles away. In between, cities filled with glass art, classic Americana, stunning waterfronts, hiking trails and award-winning restaurants and breweries are waiting for you.

Check out rhubarb pie in Sumner or antiques in Puyallup. Maritime wonder and adventure can be found in Gig Harbor, while Lakewood's majestic gardens are an oasis of calm. Discover history in Fife or connect with nature at Mount Rainier. With each just a car-ride away from Tacoma's glass art scene, it's easy to go out and explore.
